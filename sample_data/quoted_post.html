The Amputation Lottery

Another terrible and tragic patient story. I do not understand the media's focus on alleged "overuse" of revascularization procedures when patients are experiencing the Amputation Lottery. This conversation needs to change to the fact that major amputation depends on who you are, and where you live. Amputation rates vary by age, race, sex, socioeconomic status, hospital volume and geographic location, the Amputation Lottery. Amputation is overused as a "treatment" for CLI, resulting in high costs and high patient mortality and morbidity.

The patient letter compares treatment of breast cancer with their CLI treatment. Fiver-year mortality from breast cancer is 9%, from CLI 60% and from major amputation for CLI is 77%-85%.
